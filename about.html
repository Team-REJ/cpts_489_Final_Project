About CougarMarket

A campus marketplace built by WSU students, for WSU students.

What is CougarMarket?

CougarMarket is a campus-exclusive online marketplace designed to make it easy for Washington State University students to buy, sell, and trade items within the Cougar community. Whether you're looking to offload last semester's textbooks or find affordable furniture for your dorm, CougarMarket connects you with fellow students.

Unlike generic platforms like Facebook Marketplace or Craigslist, CougarMarket is built specifically for WSU students. All users are verified WSU members, making every transaction safer and more trustworthy.
