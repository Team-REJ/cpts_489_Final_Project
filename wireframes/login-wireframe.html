Role hierarchy: Student < Moderator < Admin. Students are the primary user type. Moderator and Admin pages are wireframes (planned future implementation).
Design Notes
  • Layout: vertically centered card on desktop, full-width on mobile
  • Roles: STUDENT (default), MODERATOR, ADMIN
  • No OAuth / social login in current scope
  • Session cookie: httpOnly, sameSite=lax, 30-day expiry
  • Suspended users see specific error: "Your account has been suspended"
  • On login success: redirect to role-appropriate dashboard
