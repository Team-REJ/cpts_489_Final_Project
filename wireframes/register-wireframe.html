Design Notes
  • Mobile: single-column stack, full-width inputs
  • Desktop: centered card, max-width 480px
  • All new users get STUDENT role — cannot self-promote
  • Duplicate email: clear error from server (HTTP 409)
  • No email verification flow yet — planned for Sprint 3
  • No reCAPTCHA yet — planned for Sprint 4
  • Password is hashed with bcryptjs (10 rounds) server-side
